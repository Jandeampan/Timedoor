About SUMONECETI

Sumoneceti is a unique and delicious dessert made from ingredients like almond milk, vanilla, and chocolate chips. This simple yet flavorful dessert is perfect for those looking for a refreshing and enjoyable treat.

Instructions

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed dark chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
